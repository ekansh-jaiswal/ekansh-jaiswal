## About me

I am a computer science student focused on building solid fundamentals in **systems, Linux, C++, and cybersecurity**.

My work on GitHub is mostly shaped by hands-on learning:
- solving **OverTheWire** wargames
- maintaining and refining my **Linux dotfiles**
- practicing **C++** through structured notes and exercises
- documenting what I learn in **cybersecurity**
